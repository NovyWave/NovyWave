Key Observations
  • width: 100% - Good for consistent backgrounds, but wide content gets clipped backgrounds
  • width: fit-content/max-content - Good for wide content, but leaves gaps on narrow items
  • width: 100%; min-width: max-content - Perfect hybrid: fills container AND expands for wide content
  • Background rendering is crucial for TreeView selection states and hover effects
  • The combined approach (width: 100%; min-width: max-content) is ideal for TreeView components
